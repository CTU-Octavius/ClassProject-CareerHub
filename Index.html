About Us

CareerPath is an online platform designed to help students navigate the world of careers and job opportunities. Whether you're exploring potential career paths, looking for your next internship, or applying for a full-time position, we are here to provide the resources and connections to help you succeed.

Our mission is to empower students with the right tools and knowledge to make informed decisions about their future, connect with potential employers, and gain hands-on experience through internships and jobs.
